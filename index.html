Brief Description of our project

Welcome to our interactive Black Hole photography and lightlike geodesics simulator. This project was developed for the McGill Physics Hackathon 2022! Here we have implemented massless particles propagation simulations around a Schwarszchild Black Hole, following what can be found in the literature, and we combined it with a simple and intuitive inferface built with PyQT5.
